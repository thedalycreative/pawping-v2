Each tag features a unique QR code linking to your pet's profile — name, photo, your contact info, medical needs, and more. Anyone with a phone can scan it instantly.

Apple Find My Built In

How It Works

Set up in
under 2 minutes.

No tech wizardry required. If you can take a selfie, you can protect your pet.

1

Order & Personalise

Choose your tag style, pick a colour, and enter your pet's details. We'll engrave their name and generate a unique QR code.

2

Connect & Attach

Pair your tracker with Apple Find My in one tap. Clip or thread the tag onto your pet's collar. That's it — you're live.

3

Track & Relax

See your pet's real-time location on your iPhone. If they're ever found, a quick QR scan gives the finder everything they need.
